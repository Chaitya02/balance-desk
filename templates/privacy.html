01

Introduction

Balance Desk is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our expense tracking service.

02

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: your name, email address, and password when you register.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and timestamps of activity.
03

How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the Balance Desk service.
  • Authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • Improve the app based on usage patterns.
  • Communicate with you about updates or support issues.
04

Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

05

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted service providers who assist in operating the service, provided they agree to keep your information confidential.

06

Cookies

Balance Desk uses session cookies to keep you logged in. These are essential for the service to function and do not track you across other websites.

07

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data at any time through your account settings. You may also request a copy of your data by contacting us directly.

08

Children's Privacy

Balance Desk is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

09

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting a notice on the site. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions about your privacy?

We take data protection seriously and are happy to help.
