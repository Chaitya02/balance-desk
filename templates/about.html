About Balance Desk

A simple home for your money story — built for people who want clarity, not spreadsheets.

Balance Desk started with a familiar frustration: at the end of every month the money was gone, and nobody could quite say where. Banking apps show transactions; they don't show patterns. We wanted something that answers the real question — "where does my money actually go?" — without turning budgeting into a second job.

So Balance Desk keeps the core loop deliberately small. Log an expense in seconds, give it a category, and let the dashboard do the thinking: monthly summaries, category breakdowns, and date-range views that make your habits visible at a glance. No jargon, no forced budgets, no guilt trips.

And then there's Dex — the AI companion who lives in your dashboard. Ask it anything about your spending in plain English, or tell it what you bought and it logs the expense for you. Tracking is free forever; Dex is an optional subscription for when you want a second brain on your finances.

Balance Desk is built and maintained by Chaitya Dobariya. It's a small product with a simple promise: your data stays yours, and the tracker stays free.
