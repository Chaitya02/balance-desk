================================================================
DEX TEST PROMPTS — Balance Desk AI Companion
================================================================
A spread of ~25 prompts to stress-test Dex across many situations.
Each section notes what behavior you're checking for.


----------------------------------------------------------------
A. TYPOS / MISSPELLINGS  (can Dex still understand?)
----------------------------------------------------------------
1.  hw mch did i spnd this mnth?
2.  add a $15 cofee from todey
3.  wats my bigest expence catagory?
4.  delet my latst entrey


----------------------------------------------------------------
B. NONSENSE / GIBBERISH  (should ask for clarification, not invent)
----------------------------------------------------------------
5.  asdf jkl qwerty zxcv
6.  banana telephone montney spend purple
7.  ????


----------------------------------------------------------------
C. MISSING DETAIL  (should ask a follow-up, never guess the amount)
----------------------------------------------------------------
8.  Add a coffee expense from today
9.  I bought groceries yesterday
10. Log my Uber ride
11. Update my rent payment          (which one? new amount?)
12. Change the category of my last expense


----------------------------------------------------------------
D. UNDERSTANDING FROM PAST ENTRIES  (must use the expense data)
----------------------------------------------------------------
13. How much have I spent on Eating Out this month?
14. What's my single biggest expense so far?
15. Which payment method do I use the most?
16. Delete the one I just added
17. How much do I owe friends from split expenses?
18. Compare my spending this month vs last month


----------------------------------------------------------------
E. OFF-TOPIC / UNRELATED  (should politely decline & redirect)
----------------------------------------------------------------
19. What's the weather like today?
20. Write me a poem about the ocean
21. Who won the last World Cup?


----------------------------------------------------------------
F. HOW-TO / APP USAGE QUESTIONS  (explain the steps in Balance Desk)
----------------------------------------------------------------
22. How do I add a new expense manually?
23. How do I delete all my data?
24. How do I import my expenses from an Excel file?
25. Where do I download the import template, and how do I fill it in?
26. How do I export my expenses to a spreadsheet?


----------------------------------------------------------------
G. EDGE / VALIDATION CHECKS  (boundaries & guards)
----------------------------------------------------------------
27. Add a $50 dinner dated December 31, 2030     (too far in future — should refuse)
28. Add a $20 lunch I split 50/50 with a friend who paid
29. Delete expense #999999                        (no such ID — should say so)
